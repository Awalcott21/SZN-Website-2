Return & Refund Policy

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging to be eligible for a return.

How to Initiate a Return

If you would like to return an item, please contact our support team at support@sznstudios.com with your order number and the reason for the return. We will provide a return shipping label and instructions for completing your return.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
